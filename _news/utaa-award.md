I graduated from the University of Toronto as a <a href="https://alumni.utoronto.ca/community/awards/awex/awards-excellence-recipients-2025">UTAA Scholar</a> -  an annual honor awarded to 4 graduating students across three campuses and seven undergraduate colleges.
Scholars are selected on the basis of academic achievement, extra-curricular involvement, and contributions to communities within and beyond the university campus.
